Project Overview

Restoring a Symbol of
CET's Legacy

The Diamond Jubilee Hall has been the heart of CET's community life for decades — hosting annual alumni meets, cultural festivals, technical symposiums, convocations, and countless moments that have shaped the memories of every CETian.

Today, this iconic venue needs a comprehensive renovation to meet modern standards and serve future generations with the comfort and functionality they deserve. The proposed renovation project includes:

  • ✦ Complete Air Conditioning — Installing a state-of-the-art centralized HVAC system to make the hall comfortable for year-round use in Kerala's tropical climate.
  • ✦ Ancillary Space Modernization — Upgrading green rooms, lobbies, restrooms, backstage areas, and AV infrastructure for a seamless event experience.
  • ✦ Seating & Acoustic Upgrades — Ergonomic seating, improved acoustics, and modern stage lighting to elevate the experience for every event.
  • ✦ Accessibility & Safety — Ramps, accessible seating, improved fire safety, and emergency systems to ensure inclusivity for all.

This is not just a renovation — it's an investment in the next 60 years of CET's legacy. Every alumnus who has walked through those doors has a stake in this transformation.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to the Diamond Jubilee Hall renovation? +
You can contribute by choosing one of our tier plans (Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum) or by contacting the CETAA Secretary directly at secretary@cetaa.com or +91 471 2590059. Custom contribution amounts are welcome. All contributions are tax-deductible under applicable Indian law.
What will the funds be used for specifically? +
The funds will primarily go towards: (1) Installation of a centralized air conditioning / HVAC system, (2) Modernization of ancillary spaces including green rooms, lobbies, and AV systems, (3) Seating and acoustic upgrades, (4) Accessibility improvements including ramps and safety systems. A detailed budget breakdown is available upon request from CETAA.
Will donor names be permanently displayed? +
Yes! All donors will be recognized on a digital Donor Wall inside the renovated hall. Silver tier and above donors will have their names on a physical plaque. Gold tier donors get a dedicated named seat, and Platinum donors may have spaces within the hall named after them.
Can alumni from outside India contribute? +
Absolutely! We welcome contributions from alumni worldwide. CETAA can facilitate international transactions. Please reach out to secretary@cetaa.com for bank transfer details and any currency conversion assistance. Many of our chapters across the globe are also organizing fundraising events.
When is the renovation expected to be completed? +
The renovation is planned in phases, with the HVAC installation and core renovations expected to be completed within 18 months of reaching the fundraising target. The project timeline will be regularly updated and shared with all donors. We aim to hold the grand inauguration during the next CETAA Annual Day.
